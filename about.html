About Us
Taravel is a user-friendly web application designed to assist commuters navigating within the Dasmarinas Cavite area. Offering a comprehensive guide, Taravel presents users with various routes and transportation options, empowering them with the knowledge needed to efficiently reach their destinations. Whether it's finding the fastest, cheapest, and simplest jeepney or tricycle route or exploring alternative transport modes, Taravel ensures travelers are well-informed throughout their journey.

As of now, Taravel covers the following areas:

  • Salawag
  • Paliparan III
  • Paliparan II
  • Sampaloc IV
  • Sampaloc 1
  • San Agustin II
  • Zone IV
  • Burol
  • Burol III
  • Salitran
  • and some portion of Area 1, C, D, E, F, and G
Taravel's Features

1. Route Planning: Users can input their current location and destination to receive detailed directions for their commute. This feature may include options for multiple modes of transportation, such as walking and public transit such as jeepney or tricycle.

2. Real-Time Traffic Updates: Integration with traffic data sources allows the application to provide real-time updates on traffic conditions along the chosen route.

3. Public Transit Integration: The application can provide comprehensive information on public transportation options, including jeepney or tricycle routes, fares, signboard, distance, and estimated time.

4. Interactive Maps: Interactive maps with zoom and pan capabilities allow users to visualize their route and surrounding areas easily. Users can explore points of interest along the way, view street-level imagery, and interact with the map to adjust their route as needed.

5. Multi-Platform Accessibility: The web application should be accessible across various devices and platforms, including desktop computers, smartphones, and tablets. Responsive design ensures an optimal user experience regardless of the device used.
